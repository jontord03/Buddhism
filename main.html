BUDDHISM

Overview

The Study of Buddhism is arguably just as important as its practice. Buddhism has an extensive written and oral history stretching back millenia. This website seeks to give a general overview of its history, philosphy, and practice.
I provide a brief introduction to the broad concepts of Buddhism: it's history including the story of the Buddha, the two foundational philosophical concepts of the Four Noble Truths and The Noble Eightfold Path, and finally the basic meditation practices.
I am by no means an expert in teaching Buddhist concepts, nor do I identify as a regularly practicing Buddhist. However, I am currently enrolled in an Introduction to the Study of Buddhism course at my four-year university. I will be drawing from the concepts I have learned and providing my own interpretations of certain concepts. I have provided additional resources such as a link to my Buddhism professor, Jacob Dalton, a Khyentse Foundation Distinguished University Professor in Tibetan Buddhism.
